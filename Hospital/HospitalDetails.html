Hospital Management System

This project represents a hospital management system designed to manage hospital operations efficiently. It helps in organizing patient records, doctor information, appointments, and billing details.

Patient Management

The system stores patient details such as personal information, medical history, and treatment records. This helps doctors access accurate patient data quickly.

Doctor Management

The hospital management system maintains doctor information including specialization, availability, and contact details.

Appointment Scheduling

Patients can book appointments with doctors based on availability. The system helps reduce waiting time and manages schedules efficiently.

Medical Records

The system keeps track of patient diagnoses, prescriptions, and laboratory reports for future reference.

Billing and Payments

The billing module calculates treatment costs, generates bills, and manages payment records in an organized manner.
