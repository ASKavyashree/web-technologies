Travel Booking System

This project describes an online platform where users can book flights, hotels, and holiday packages easily. It helps customers plan their trips in a convenient and efficient way.

Flight Booking

The system allows users to search for available flights based on source, destination, and travel date. Customers can compare prices and select suitable options.

Hotel Reservation

Users can explore different hotels, check room availability, and book accommodations according to their budget and preferences.

Holiday Packages

The platform provides customized holiday packages including transportation, hotel stay, and sightseeing services.

Online Payment

The system supports secure online payments through credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets.

Booking Management

Customers can view, modify, or cancel their bookings through their user account dashboard.
